This poem does or does not reflect my current feelings

In a room, doomed of loneliness
With light switched off to darkness,
My shadow, scattered on the wall.
I am well trying to balance
How I ended feeling so dull in my soul.
There is no music playing, no people talking, no children playing around.
Its just me and my shadow.
At some point, I wish it could literally
Lend me its ears,
So that it can know how pierced is my heart.
